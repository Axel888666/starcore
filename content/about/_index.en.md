# About StellerCore Technology

## Company Overview

StellerCore Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. is a professional big data infrastructure software platform provider, committed to providing reliable big data solutions and services for enterprises. With the mission of "Data-driven, Achieving the Future", the company focuses on bringing advanced big data technology to every industry and organization, building a data-driven intelligent future.

## Development History

### 2013 - Company Establishment
The company was established in Shenzhen, focusing on big data infrastructure software development

### 2015 - Product Launch
Released the first generation big data platform product, gaining market recognition

### 2018 - Business Expansion
Business coverage expanded to finance, manufacturing, energy and other industries

### 2020 - Technology Upgrade
Launched new generation cloud-native big data platform

### 2023 - Global Layout
Established global service network, serving international customers

### 2024 - Innovation Breakthrough
Released StellerCore Enterprise V9.1, leading the industry in technology
